Interpretation

Composite Score Formula

raw = sum( lever_score[i] × weight[i] )
Weights: Quality 25%, Productivity 20%, Cost 20%, Revenue 20%, Risk 15%

feasibility = ( dataReadiness + (6 − techComplexity) + (6 − regulatoryRisk) ) / 15

composite = min(100, round( raw × 16 + feasibility × 20 ))

Lever scores range 1–5. Feasibility inputs range 1–5. Higher feasibility scores reflect better data readiness and lower complexity/risk.
