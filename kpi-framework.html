A comprehensive KPI map organized by value lever — covering 20+ metrics from cycle time and error rate to findings resolution and sponsor NPS.

Note on KPI selection
KPIs shown are illustrative examples. Adjust selection to match your use case during intake. Not every KPI applies to every use case — pick 3–5 primary KPIs that can realistically be tracked pre/post deployment.
