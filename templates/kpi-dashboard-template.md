# KPI Dashboard Template

> Monthly performance tracking for active AI and automation initiatives.

## Instructions

This template is completed by the Program Manager in collaboration with the initiative sponsor and analytics team. It is submitted to the PMO lead by the **5th business day of each month** for the prior month's data.

For each initiative, maintain one instance of this template. Archive monthly snapshots to preserve trend history.
