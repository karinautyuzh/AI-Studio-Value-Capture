KPI Library

80+ KPI definitions across six value domains — each with definition, type, calculation method, and data source guidance. Select 1–3 primary KPIs per initiative before launch.

KPI Selection Rules

  • → Select 1–3 primary KPIs directly driven by the initiative
  • → Add 1–3 secondary KPIs for adjacent value or early signals
  • → Every KPI needs a baseline before launch
  • → Include at least one quantitative KPI

Selection Guidance

Choosing the Right KPIs

  • Select KPIs that are directly influenced by the AI initiative — not tangentially
  • Choose metrics with an existing data source and baseline before launch
  • Include at least one leading indicator (early signal) and one lagging indicator (final outcome)
  • Limit primary KPIs to 3 maximum to maintain accountability
  • Confirm the data owner can provide the metric on the required frequency
    • KPI Tiers

    Reporting Tiers

    TierPurposeFrequency
    Tier 1 — PrimaryThe 1–2 KPIs the initiative is primarily accountable forMonthly
    Tier 2 — SecondarySupporting metrics indicating initiative healthQuarterly
    Tier 3 — MonitorBackground metrics to watch for unintended effectsQuarterly or as needed
