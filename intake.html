Intake & Prioritization Framework

A repeatable, transparent process for submitting, evaluating, and prioritizing AI and automation opportunities — from first submission to governance decision.

Foundation

Framework Principles

⚖️
Consistency
All submissions go through the same intake process regardless of origin, function, or seniority of the submitter.
🔍
Transparency
Scoring criteria and weights are published and visible to all stakeholders. Decisions are documented and traceable.
🌐
Inclusivity
Use cases may be submitted from any function: clinical, operational, finance, IT, or strategy. Good ideas come from everywhere.
⚡
Speed
The process moves quickly from submission to decision. Typical cycle: 3–6 weeks from complete submission to governance decision.
💎
Value Orientation
Every prioritized initiative must have a defined value hypothesis and measurable KPIs before resources are committed.
🛡️
Risk Awareness
Clinical safety, regulatory risk, and equity considerations are evaluated alongside value potential — not as afterthoughts.

Any employee, leader, or external partner may submit using the Intake Form. All submissions require an identified executive sponsor.

A complete submission must include:

  • Problem statement and current-state description
  • Proposed AI/automation solution concept
  • Affected stakeholder groups and estimated scale
  • Initial hypothesis on value (cost, quality, experience, revenue, workforce, or risk)
  • Sponsoring leader name and confirmation of support
  • Data availability self-assessment
  • Known technical or regulatory dependencies

Portfolio Management

Portfolio Balancing Criteria

The Governance Committee considers the portfolio composition — not just individual scores — when making prioritization decisions.

CriterionGuideline
Capacity balanceNo more than 40% of active initiatives in any single value domain
Horizon balanceMix of short-term (0–6 mo), medium-term (6–18 mo), and transformational (18+ mo)
Risk balanceHigh-complexity/high-risk initiatives limited to 20% of active portfolio
Sponsor coverageAdequate executive sponsor bandwidth across all approved initiatives
