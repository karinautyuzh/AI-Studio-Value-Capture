## Section 2: Problem Statement

### 2.1 Current State Problem
_Describe the clinical or operational problem this AI use case addresses.
Be specific: What is happening today? What is the impact (patient, staff, financial)?
Example: "ED physicians lack early warning for sepsis onset, resulting in delayed treatment initiation for ~15% of septic patients, contributing to excess mortality and ICU LOS."_

### 2.2 Proposed AI Solution
_Describe the AI/ML approach at a high level.
What type of AI? (predictive model, NLP, computer vision, generative AI, optimization, etc.)
What does it do, when, and for whom?_

### 2.3 Scope
- **Patient Population:** _Who does this affect? (e.g., all inpatients, ED patients >18 years)_
- **Care Setting:** _Where is this used? (ED, ICU, ambulatory, revenue cycle, etc.)_
- **Users:** _Who interacts with the AI output? (physicians, nurses, coders, schedulers)_
- **Volume:** _Estimated volume impacted (patients/day, encounters/year, transactions/month)_
